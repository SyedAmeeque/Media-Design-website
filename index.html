Meet The Founder

Oliver Evans

London-born Oliver Evans is the founder, creative director and CEO of Media Design, an award-winning international design manufacturing firm with offices in Hong Kong, Shenzhen (China), Miami (USA), London (UK), and Dubai (UAE).

In 2000, aged 18, Oliver started his first company in the English capital importing electric scooters from Taiwan. Later on, he developed self-balancing technology for electric motors, and he now owns several patents for his inventions.
